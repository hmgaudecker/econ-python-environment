These pages should help researchers to get started for setting up a Python environment on their machines. The guide has grown out of some courses that I have been teaching and at some points you will find references for students of these courses. Just ignore them if you do not belong to this group.

  • If everything goes smoothly and you have a fast machine and a fast Internet connection, you should be done within half an hour.
  • Please follow the steps one-by-one in and do not deviate at all from them, unless you really know what you are doing.
  • If you find any outdated links or unclear descriptions, I would appreciate if you dropped me a line or opened an issue on Github.
